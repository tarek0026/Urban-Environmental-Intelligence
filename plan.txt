🧠 🌍 Urban Environmental Intelligence System

An integrated system to analyze and predict urban environmental factors affecting air quality and energy consumption.

🎯 Research Questions
1️⃣ Green Space → Air Quality

Does increasing green space improve air quality?

2️⃣ Weekend vs Weekday → Air Quality

Are pollution levels lower on weekends compared to weekdays?

3️⃣ Temperature → Electricity Consumption

How does temperature affect electricity consumption?

🔗 System Story (Key Insight)

Temperature → Electricity Consumption → Emissions → Air Quality

This creates a connected analytical narrative, which is very important for evaluation.
